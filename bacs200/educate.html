TED Talks

Scott Mescudi "Kid Cudi" - Hi, I'm Scott

  • Kid Cudi speaks on his stuggles and problems with his identity that plauged him in his youth.
  • Mescudi makes it a point that despite all of the things he went through his love and drive for music never wavered.
  • He also urges the audience both in person and at home to seek help for their own issues so that they don't hold back their passions.

Shawn Achor - The happy secret to better work

  • Good Think Inc. CEO, Shawn Achor speaks on the affects that positivity and happiness have on peoples work life.
  • Achor works to encourage employees in order to increase happiness and in turn increase creativity, work ability and more.
  • Achor's points can be applies to most professions, some can be very intense and knowing how to be happy can keep your productivity up!

Sir Ken Robinson- Do schools kill creativity

  • Sir Ken Robinson delivers a great talk about schools not helping kids reach their full potential.
  • Sir Robinson gives the example of a girl named Gillian who was viewed asa problemed child i school, after her mother takes her to see a doctor, they soon realize that there is nothing wrong with her, she is just a dancer.
  • Gillain now at the age of 92 looks back on a very successful dancing career, proving the school that had written her off wrong.

RSA Animate

How To Help Every Child Fulfil Their Potential

  • This RSA animation by Carol Dweck goes over the difference between a fixed and a growth mindset.
  • Dweck describes that in her research , praising students' progress while learning has a positive effect and that praising thier intelligence has mostly a negitive effect.
  • She shares the importance of developing a growth mindset in order to help students persist in the face of challenges and develop their skills.
